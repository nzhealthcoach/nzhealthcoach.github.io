Appointments

A health coaching session is 25 minutes, and costs US$40.

If you would like to talk longer, please feel free to book additional back-to-back appointments.

I usually like to meet using Skype (with video). Please add me as a contact in advance of the appointment time. My Skype ID is "kiessig."

For best audio quality, I recommend using a headset with a built-in microphone. Please double-check that your microphone is working.

If Skype doesn't work for you, let me know, and we'll find another way.

Appointments are paid in advance. After booking your appointment, please pay using the PayPal button below.
